Touchy Subjects is a sexual health and wellness organization and platform We are a non-profit, independent organization that features digestible information to youth, especially those that identify as men. TouchySubjects aims to be as inclusive as possible, and is always ready to receive feedback on how we can improve.

How we operate

We are funded in collaboration with Vancouver Coastal Health, but we operate as our own organization. The information found on our page has been stripped down from long, tedious articles and presented in a way that is respectful to you as a reader, and easy to read. Our subject matter experts take care of this, and make sure nothing is left out.

Volunteers

Our volunteers are extensively interviewed and screened so that you are getting real, professional advice from registered counsellors, nurses, and sex-experts. In addition, we collaborate with the Vancouver Coastal Health’s nurses’ hotline for a priority spot in their hotline each day.

TouchySubjects is a no judgement, safe space. You can learn more about how to get involved, or find out more on our volunteers and services here.
