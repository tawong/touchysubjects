What to expect
when you're not expecting.

Touchy Subjects is a sexual health and wellness platform, primarily targeted as those that identify as men. Informing yourself and creating a healthy perspective of sex and sexual identity is nothing to be embarrassed about — ask those “weird” questions, get the resources and knowledge you need, or find extra help — whatever it is, we’re here, no matter the subject.

You

Sex starts with you. Learn about having sex for the first time, or even all the stuff that comes before “it”. Sexual identity, hygiene, and habits are all an integral foundation to a healthy sex life.

Partner

Introducing someone else to your sexual routine is a great step. Serious relationship or not, you should always be aware of the risks you face when you involve another person. Read up on how to be sexually proactive in safetsy and consideration.

Help

Need to talk to someone? Submit a question in our F.A.Q, talk to a registered nurse and sex advisor, or find a youth clinic near you.
